The excitement is building as BeXide announces that their popular multiplayer 3D puzzle game, Fruit Mountain Party, will be making its way to the Switch 2 and Switch in spring 2026. Currently in Early Access on PC, this game has already captured the attention of many gamers with its unique blend of puzzle-solving and competitive gameplay. The Switch version will be showcased at major gaming events, including Osaka Game Dungeon and Tokyo eSports Festa 2026, giving fans a first look at what to expect.

Fruit Mountain Party invites players to engage in fruit battles, combining and stacking fruits to create bigger ones while competing against friends or players worldwide. With features like heated online battle modes and a single-player ranking system, the game promises to deliver a fun and engaging experience for all types of players. Whether you're a casual gamer or a competitive player, there's something for everyone in this vibrant fruit-filled adventure.
