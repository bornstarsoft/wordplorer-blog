Kodansha Game Lab and Octorain have unveiled TrailRail, an innovative 2D action puzzle game that promises to redefine the genre on PC via Steam. With a unique gameplay mechanic where your movement creates a tangible path, players will navigate through 38 engaging puzzles that challenge both intuition and creativity. The free demo available during Steam Next Fest allows players to experience this fresh take on puzzle-solving firsthand.

In TrailRail, you take on the role of Rail, a girl on a quest to uncover her lost memories while traversing a mysterious world. The games simple controlsMove, Jump, and Materialize Trajectorybelie the complexity of the puzzles that await. Players will need to think outside the box, using their movement trails to create bridges and footholds, pushing the boundaries of traditional puzzle mechanics. As you guide Rail through her journey, the game invites you to explore the deeper narrative of self-discovery and the unknown.

As we look forward to the full release, TrailRail stands out as a promising addition to the puzzle genre, merging engaging gameplay with a compelling story. Will you be ready to leave your mark on this world?
