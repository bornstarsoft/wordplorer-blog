Get ready to embark on an exciting journey with "Quack Quack Attack - PEGG Blaster," a new mobile puzzle game that promises to deliver a unique blend of strategy and fun. Set to launch on iOS and Android later this year, this game invites players to take on the role of Captain Quack, guiding a team of super-powered birds through a vibrant, monster-infested world.

In this engaging game, players will need to strategize their bird selections and utilize their unique abilities to clear levels by ricocheting eggs off enemies in a pachinko-style format. With up to fifty different birds across six classes, the game offers a rich variety of gameplay options, ensuring that no two sessions are the same. The pre-registration rewards are also enticing, giving players a head start with valuable in-game items upon launch.
