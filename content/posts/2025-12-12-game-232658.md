The upcoming puzzle game "Order of the Sinking Star" from Jonathan Blow promises to redefine the genre with its intricate mechanics and expansive world. With over 1,430 puzzles, players will find themselves immersed in a richly designed universe where they control a queen navigating through various themed realms, each offering unique challenges and gameplay mechanics.

As players explore the four distinct worldseach resembling a mini-gamethey will encounter a range of puzzles that require creative thinking and strategic planning. The game's design encourages exploration and non-linear progression, allowing players to tackle challenges at their own pace. With multiple endings and a vast array of secrets to uncover, it caters to both casual gamers and completionists alike.

Set to release in 2026, "Order of the Sinking Star" is already generating buzz among fans of Blow's previous works, such as "The Witness" and "Braid." Will this game elevate the puzzle genre to new heights?
