If you're seeking a tranquil escape from the chaos of everyday life, look no further than "The Cute Whale," a delightful new puzzle game that invites players to restore harmony in the ocean. Available on Xbox One, Xbox Series X|S, and Windows PC for just 4.19, this game offers a refreshing take on the classic block-pushing puzzle genre, combining strategy with relaxation.

In "The Cute Whale," players guide a lovable whale through 30 handcrafted levels across vibrant biomes like the Caribbean Sea and Coral Reef. The goal is simple yet engaging: help lost turtles find their way back to safety by carefully planning your moves. With its charming pixel art and soothing ambiance, this game is perfect for those who enjoy a moment of zen while solving clever puzzles.
