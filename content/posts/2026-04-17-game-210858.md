In a world where technology and emotions intertwine, the upcoming release of "Save My Scrap" offers players a unique blend of visual storytelling and puzzle-solving. Set to launch on Steam on April 22, 2026, this game invites players to delve into the life of a mechanic tasked with repairing an android named Harima. As you engage in the intricate mechanics of repair, youll uncover a narrative that explores deep emotional connections and forgotten memories, making it more than just a game about fixing machines.

Originally based on the popular free game "Fix My Junk," this upgraded version promises enhanced gameplay with new dialogues, endings, and a gallery mode. The games stunning cyberpunk art style and poignant storyline have already captivated audiences in Japan, and now its ready to make waves globally. With support for multiple languages and various gaming platforms, including Nintendo Switch and PlayStation 5, it aims to reach a diverse audience.

As we anticipate its release, one can't help but wonder how this game will resonate with players worldwide. Will the emotional depth and engaging puzzles create a lasting impact in the gaming community? Only time will tell, but one thing is for sure: "Save My Scrap" is poised to be a memorable experience.
