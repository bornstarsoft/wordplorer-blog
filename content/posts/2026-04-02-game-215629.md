Devolver Digital's upcoming game, Minos, is set to launch on April 9, and it promises to turn traditional gaming expectations on their head. Instead of playing as the hero, players will take on the role of a mastermind architect, tasked with constructing a living labyrinth to protect Asterion, the cursed king known as the Minotaur. This unique twist on Greek mythology not only reimagines the classic tale but also introduces a fresh gameplay dynamic that challenges players to think strategically about maze design.

In Minos, the labyrinth is more than just a backdrop; it is a sentient entity that evolves with each encounter, feeding on the blood of fallen adventurers. Players will manipulate walls, corridors, and traps in real time, creating intricate pathways to thwart invading heroes. With each playthrough offering new enemies and tactical challenges, the game ensures that no two experiences are the same. As players delve deeper into the narrative, they will uncover the tragic backstory of Asterion and the genius of Daedalus, the architect behind the maze.

With its innovative blend of strategy, puzzle mechanics, and mythological storytelling, Minos is poised to captivate gamers looking for a fresh take on classic narratives. Will you be ready to defend your labyrinth when it launches this April?
