Sonic Racing: CrossWorlds is set to delight fans with exciting new content featuring Pac-Man, launching on January 7. This update not only introduces a paid Pac-Man Pack for Season Pass holders but also includes a surprise in-game Pac-Man Festival available to all players from January 7 to January 11. This festival promises to bring a wave of nostalgia and fun to the racing experience, allowing everyone to join in on the festivities, regardless of their purchase.

The Pac-Man Pack enhances the game with unique racers and a new course, following the trend of integrating beloved franchises into Sonic Racing. With previous additions like SpongeBob SquarePants and Minecraft, Sega is clearly committed to keeping the game fresh and engaging. Moreover, the director has hinted at even more surprises throughout the year, including upcoming content based on Mega Man and Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.
