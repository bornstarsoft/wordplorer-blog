Peter Molyneux, the visionary behind the original Fable series, expressed deep emotion upon witnessing the recent gameplay reveal of Playground Games' reboot. In an interview with IGN, he candidly admitted to tearing up, reflecting on the legacy of the world he helped create. Molyneux's heartfelt reaction underscores the significance of nostalgia and the enduring impact of beloved franchises in the gaming community.

Molyneux praised Playground's decision to retell the Fable story rather than continue from the original games, which he felt could have been a burden. He highlighted clever references in the trailer, such as the transformation of a child's family into stone, showcasing the creative depth that fans have come to expect from the series. While he noted some minor criticisms regarding the aesthetic, he remains optimistic about the game's potential to capture the chaotic essence of the original Fable universe.
