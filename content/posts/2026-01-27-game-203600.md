In an exciting turn of events, Walmart may have inadvertently revealed two unannounced GameCube titles set to join the Nintendo Switch Online library. The retailer's website displayed an image showcasing Metroid Prime 2 and Pikmin 2 among the available games, sparking speculation among fans about their imminent release.

Currently, the Nintendo Switch Online service offers a limited selection of GameCube titles, with only eight games available. While Nintendo has confirmed three upcoming titlesSuper Mario Sunshine, Pokmon XD: Gale of Darkness, and Pokmon Colosseumno specific release dates have been announced. The potential inclusion of Metroid Prime 2 and Pikmin 2 could significantly enhance the library, especially considering the nostalgia these classics evoke.

As we await official confirmation from Nintendo, this leak raises questions about the future of GameCube games on the platform. Will we see more beloved titles make their way to the Switch Online service? Only time will tell, but the excitement among fans is palpable.
