If you're a fan of puzzle games but often find them too challenging, then you'll be excited to hear about Botany Manor, which is currently free on the Epic Games Store. This game strikes a perfect balance between engaging puzzles and a relaxed atmosphere, making it ideal for casual gamers who enjoy a bit of mental stimulation without the frustration.

In Botany Manor, you step into the shoes of botanist Arabella Greene, exploring a Victorian manor filled with botanical mysteries. Unlike other puzzle games that can leave you feeling defeated, Botany Manor is designed to be accessible while still offering enough intrigue to keep you invested. Lead developer Laure De May emphasized that the game is crafted to be peaceful and enjoyable, focusing on exploration rather than punishing difficulty.

This unique approach has garnered positive feedback, with players appreciating its cozy vibe and inviting puzzles. If youre looking for a game that allows you to unwind while still engaging your mind, Botany Manor is definitely worth checking out. Will you take the plunge into this charming puzzle adventure?
