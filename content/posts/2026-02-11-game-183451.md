Apple Arcade is set to expand its gaming library with the highly anticipated release of "Oceanhorn 3: Legend of the Shadow Sea" on March 5, 2026. This new installment in the beloved action-RPG-adventure franchise takes players nearly a thousand years after the events of its predecessor, inviting both fans and newcomers to explore enchanting islands, battle titans, and uncover the mysteries of a world poised for rebirth. The game promises a stunning 3D experience, available across multiple Apple devices, including iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple TV, and Apple Vision Pro.

In addition to Oceanhorn 3, Apple Arcade will also welcome three other exciting titles on the same day: "Pocket Love!+", a charming life simulator; "Flow Free+", a challenging puzzle game; and "Doraemon Dorayaki Shop Story+", a delightful shop management game featuring the iconic manga character. These new additions are sure to offer something for every type of gamer, enhancing the already diverse Apple Arcade catalog.

As Apple Arcade continues to evolve, it remains a compelling option for gamers looking for a subscription service that provides access to a wide variety of titles. With a monthly fee of $6.99 and a free trial available, its an enticing choice for those eager to dive into new gaming adventures.
