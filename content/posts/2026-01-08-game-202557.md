The recently launched game AiliA is captivating players with its innovative mirror-based gameplay mechanics. Developed by IceToad Studio LLC and published by Gamirror Games, this 3D puzzle game invites players to step into the shoes of Aria, a young girl navigating through a world where reality and reflection intertwine. With over 100 puzzles spread across seven distinct themed areas, AiliA promises a unique gaming experience that challenges the mind and engages the senses.

What sets AiliA apart is its interactive mirror mechanics that allow players to manipulate their environment in real-time. Unlike traditional puzzle games, mirrors in AiliA are not just visual reflections; they are gateways to new paths and solutions. This innovative approach to puzzle-solving, combined with stunning visuals and immersive storytelling, makes AiliA a must-try for puzzle enthusiasts and casual gamers alike.

As players delve into the mysteries of AiliA, they will uncover the intertwined stories of sisters Aria and Lia, making every puzzle solved a step closer to revealing their hidden memories. With a free demo available on Steam, now is the perfect time to explore this enchanting world. Will you take on the challenge and discover the secrets that lie within?
