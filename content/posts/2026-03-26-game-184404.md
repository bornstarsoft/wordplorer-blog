In an exciting development for retro gaming enthusiasts, Hyperkin has teamed up with ModRetro to unveil a line of color-matched premium controllers for the upcoming M64 console. These controllers, known as the Captain Premium Controllers, are designed to withstand the rigors of intense gameplay while providing a comfortable grip for gamers. With a robust 3-meter cable, players can easily navigate their gaming spaces without feeling tethered.

The new Captain Premium Controllers will be available in vibrant colors like Green, Clear, Purple, and Red, alongside existing options. This collaboration not only enhances the aesthetic appeal of the M64 console but also promises to elevate the gaming experience with features such as an extension port for memory cards and rumble feedback accessories. As Slade Dude Suzuki, Lead Product Developer for Hyperkin, stated, this partnership is a dream come true for both teams, reflecting their passion for retro gaming.

As we look forward to the release of these controllers later this year, one can't help but wonder how they will reshape the landscape of retro gaming. Will these innovations rekindle the nostalgia of the 64-bit era for a new generation of gamers?
