This December, PlayStation Plus subscribers are in for a treat with the addition of blockbuster titles like **Assassins Creed Mirage** and **Wo Long: Fallen Dynasty** to the Game Catalog. These titles are set to enhance the gaming experience for both Extra and Premium tier members, ensuring that players have access to some of the most exciting games available today.

In addition to these major releases, the catalog will also feature the newly launched **Skate Story**, along with family-friendly options like **Paw Patrol** games and **Lego Horizon Adventures**. For Premium members, the classic fighter **Soulcalibur III** joins the Classic Catalog, bringing nostalgia and competitive gameplay back to the forefront.
